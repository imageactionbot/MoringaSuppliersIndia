Import playbook

Moringa import compliance checklist for USA and Europe

Use this before final payment: documentation quality usually decides margin more than FOB negotiation.

This page is designed for US/EU buyers sourcing India-origin moringa powder, tea cut, oil, or seeds. Use it as an operating checklist, not legal advice.

Decision table: what to verify first

AreaMinimum evidenceWhy it matters
Entity identityIEC/GST name chain + bank consistencyPrevents invoice/entity mismatch risk
Lot qualityLot-specific COA + method referencesAvoids marketing PDF-only approvals
Organic claimsScope lines tied to product + siteReduces certification claim exposure
Contract protectionArrival retest + deviation clauseControls post-arrival dispute cost

US-focused checks

  • Align claims language to marketplace and label policy before launch.
  • Ask for heavy metals and microbiology panel scope in writing.
  • Specify arrival testing workflow and tolerance thresholds.

EU-focused checks

  • Define contaminant and pesticide expectation before sample approval.
  • Confirm organic paperwork continuity across the transaction path.
  • Ensure label panel, lot coding, and pack traceability stay consistent.

Ready-to-use checklist sequence

  1. Freeze SKU and moisture/particle specs.
  2. Run identity + document chain check.
  3. Approve production-path sample only.
  4. Issue pilot PO with retest clause.
  5. Scale only after pilot passes arrival QA.
