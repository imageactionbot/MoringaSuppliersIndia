Problem-first: why MOQ talks fail

Most buyers ask for the best price too early. Suppliers respond with generic catalog rates, and both sides lose time.

Decision table: sample path

Evidence-based checklist before MOQ commitment

  1. Sample corresponds to the same production path used for pilot.
  2. COA references the same lot or adjacent documented lots.
  3. Supplier accepts arrival retest and deviation language.
  4. Payment milestones are tied to inspection events.
  5. Timeline includes realistic customs and lab windows.

Short negotiation script

"We can move to pilot quantities after sample verification. Please share lot-level documents, target MOQ bands, and milestone payment terms for a first shipment to our destination port."
