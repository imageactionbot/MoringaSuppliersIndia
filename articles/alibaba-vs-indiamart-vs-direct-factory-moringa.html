Buyer strategy

Alibaba vs IndiaMART vs direct factory outreach for moringa

Choose channel by job, not habit. Good sourcing starts with the right first touchpoint.

Quick decision matrix

ChannelBest forWatch-outs
AlibabaWorldwide B2B, machines + ingredient discoveryDo not skip lot-level diligence
IndiaMARTIndia supplier density, domestic RFQ velocityFilter aggressively for documentation quality
Direct factoryRepeat orders after trust is establishedHigher initial outreach effort

By buyer profile

  • US/EU importer: start with Alibaba + checklist discipline; add IndiaMART to compare India pricing and responsiveness.
  • India brand builder: start with IndiaMART and local references; use Alibaba when machine/vendor options are limited.
  • Established procurement team: move to direct factory lane after two to three successful documented cycles.

Verification does not change across channels

Regardless of channel, apply the same stack: entity match, cert scope, production-path sample, lot-specific COA, and arrival retest clauses.
